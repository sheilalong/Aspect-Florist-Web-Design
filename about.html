Our Story

We are committed to continuously innovate while offering high-quality floral arrangements, gifts, and friendly, prompt service. We cater to a wide range of industries, including fashion, entertainment, advertising, finance, publishing, and hospitality. This deep love of flowers and plants drives our vision, and it's what keeps us focused on what we do.

Aspect florists are a contemporary florist that creates innovative and attention-grabbing arrangements using fresh flowers for clients looking for something unique and special. Our team understands how important it is for every occasion to convey the right emotions. As a result, we carefully source and select sculptural botanical elements, exotic seed pods, rare tropical foliage, and original arrangements that are uniquely tailored to your style.

The concept of eco-friendliness is incorporated into all aspects of Aspect's projects, from our flowers and products to our materials and practices. Our love of native flowers inspired us to include free wildflower packets in bouquets as a memento of their special day and be reminded of the occasion for the years to come. Our philosophy is to use native plants and seeds to cultivate native plants in our gardens, which will attract birds and other wildlife. It is often overlooked that flowers are an important part of eco-friendly decor. Hence, we have made going green easy for eco-conscious companies, event planners, individuals, and even brides.

Belinda Aspect

Founder

While pursuing a college degree in Urban Studies with an emphasis in Public Administration at TAFE, Belinda pursued her passion for flowers as part-time work at a local flower shop and occasionally worked in a friend's flower shop. Her passion bloomed when she moved to New York after graduating. Her portfolio included a half dozen weddings, including her own. Her passion for floral design and sustainability has motivated her to begin her green efforts.

Tina has been a key team member in all aspects of floral processing, shop maintenance, and event installation and strike.
